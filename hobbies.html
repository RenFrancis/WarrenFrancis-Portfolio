What I love to do

Photography

I love to take photos of environments and animals! This photo won me the "Best capture of Autumn" photography competition in north west Japan.

Caring for wildlife and nature

I absolutely have a soft spot for all animals and I love attending nature walks. I enjoy gardening too!

Card games

I love competitive card games. I enjoy the complexity of the puzzles and problem solving they provide. I am an official Konami judge for the card game Yu-Gi-Oh! I give Konami a volentary hand from time to time to help maintain their tournament events with expert knowledge of the card game rulings.

Cooking

I have a passion for learning dishes that are not native to the U.K. I enjoy making food all from scratch and host dinners during the weekends for friends and family.

Fighting games

It all started with Street Fighter II and I have never stopped playing fighting games since! I am incredibly passionate for fighting games and even secured an official E-Sports sponsor in the past for my passion! This genre is what formed my love for gaming communities and has provided me with some of my fondest video game memories.

Japanese language and world culture

I can speak, read and write Japanese! I spent two years of my career living in Niigata, Japan! It was also an exciting time being a local to Tokyo! I now have a passion for learning world culture and picking up additional languages!

Car racing and driving

I love racing sports! I am a regular at Silverstone racetrack. I have a keen passion for driving too and enjoy drives across mountains. However, I am always a responsible driver.

Helping local charity

More of a moral duty than a hobby but always happy to help in any way I can. I try to aid the homeless as often as possible. This includes hosting gaming events with the community to raise funds, directly helping the homeless myself or working with support staff to help those in need.
